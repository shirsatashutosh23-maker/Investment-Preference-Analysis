# Investment-Preference-Analysis
Investor Behavior & Preference Analysis Dashboard

This project focuses on analyzing investor demographics, behavior patterns, and investment preferences using an interactive dashboard built in Power BI. The dataset includes key variables such as age, gender, investment avenues (Equity, Mutual Funds, Fixed Deposits, PPF), and behavioral factors like risk appetite, investment objectives, and decision drivers.
The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of investor trends through multiple visualizations, enabling better understanding of how different demographic groups make financial decisions.

**⚙️ Key Work Done:**

Performed data cleaning and transformation using Power Query
Built interactive dashboards with filters (gender, age group, investment type)

**Created KPIs such as:**

Average Age of Investors (28)
% of Equity Investors (25%)
Total Investors (40)

**Designed visuals including:**

Age group distribution
Investment avenue preference (Mutual Funds, Equity, FD, PPF)
Risk factor analysis
Investment duration vs avenue
Source of investment awareness
Investment objectives and purposes

**📈 Key Insights:**

Majority of investors fall under 21–30 age group, indicating higher participation from young investors
Mutual Funds are the most preferred investment option, followed by Fixed Deposits and Equity
Returns are the primary decision-making factor, while locking period has minimal impact
Most investors aim for capital appreciation, followed by growth and income
Financial consultants and newspapers are the dominant sources of investment awareness
Investment duration is commonly between 1–5 years, showing mid-term investment preference

**🎯 Business Impact:**

This dashboard helps in:
Understanding customer segmentation
Identifying investment trends and preferences
Supporting financial institutions in designing targeted investment products and strategies
